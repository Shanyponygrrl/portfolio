About

Living & Breathing Content

I'm Shany Levy, 35, from Tel Aviv-Jaffa. For about 8 years I worked at the Tel Aviv-Jaffa Municipality Spokesperson's Office, where I founded Israel's first municipal podcast and led the development of the podcasting department. I also managed all digital marketing for the city's youth division.

Today I run Pony Studio — a content creation, marketing consulting, and podcast production studio for businesses and social organizations. I help business owners show up online authentically, without feeling like they're being fake.

BA in Communications, Google & Meta certified, with over a decade of hands-on experience in digital marketing and advertising.
